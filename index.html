What I Learned About the Background Image CSS Element

Using a background image with background-size: cover taught me that the image automatically scales to completely fill the viewport while maintaining its aspect ratio. I also learned that combining a fixed background with a semi-transparent overlay (via a pseudo-element) helps ensure that the content remains readable, regardless of the image's colors or details, even as the image stays stationary during scrolling.
